​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​​‍‍​​​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍The Caribbean islands are divided into two worlds, a rich one and a poor one. This tropical region's economy is based mainly on farming. // 加勒比海岛屿分为两个世界——富人的世界和穷人的世界。这个热带地区的经济主要以农业为基础。
Farmers are of two types. One is the plantation owner who may have hundreds of thousands of acres. In contrast, the small cultivator is working only a few acres of land. // 农民有两种类型。一种是可能拥有数十万英亩土地的种植园主。相比之下，小农只耕种几英亩土地。
Most visitors to the Caribbean are rich, like the plantation owner. They do not realize or do not want to realize that many farm families barely managed to get by on what they grow. // 大多数到加勒比海旅游的富人，像种植园主一样。他们没有意识到或不想意识到许多农家靠种的东西勉强维持生计。
The Caribbean produces many things. Sugar is the main product. Other export crops are tobacco, coffee, bananas, spices, and citrus fruits, such as orange, lemon or grapefruit. // 加勒比海出产很多东西。糖是主要产品。其他出口作物包括烟草、咖啡、香蕉、香料和柑橘类水果（如橙子、柠檬或葡萄柚）。
From the West Indies also come oil, mineral pitch, and many forest products. Jamaica's aluminum ore supplies are the world's largest. Oil comes from Trinidad, Aruba and Curacao. But for many of the smaller islands, sugar is the only export. // 西印度群岛也出产石油、沥青和许多林产品。牙买加的铝土矿供应量是世界最大的。石油来自特立尼达、阿鲁巴和库拉索。但对于许多较小的岛屿来说，糖是唯一的出口产品。
Rum, a strong alcoholic drink which is distilled from sugar cane, is also an export. The world's best rum comes from this area. Local种类 vary from the light rums of Puerto Rico to the heavier, darker rums of Barbados and Jamaica. // 朗姆酒是一种用甘蔗蒸馏的烈性酒精饮料，也是出口品。世界上最好的朗姆酒产自这一地区。当地品种从波多黎各的清淡朗姆酒到巴巴多斯和牙买加的浓重深色朗姆酒不等。
American tourists enjoy stocking up on inexpensive high-quality Caribbean rum while they're on vacation. In Curacao, the well-known liquor of that name is made from the thick outer skin of a native orange. // 美国游客喜欢在度假时囤积物美价廉的高品质加勒比朗姆酒。在库拉索岛，那种以该地名命名的著名利口酒是用当地橙子的厚外皮制成的。
Ever since America's colonial days, the Caribbean islands have been favorite places to visit. Since World War II, tourism has increased rapidly. // 从美国殖民时代起，加勒比海岛屿就是人们喜爱的旅游目的地。第二次世界大战以来，旅游业迅速增长。
Because great numbers of people go there, the islanders have built elaborate resorts, developed harbors and airfields, improved beaches and have expanded sea and air routes. Everything is at the resort — hotel, beach, shopping and recreation. The vacationer never has any reason to explore the island. // 因为大量游客前往那里，岛上居民建造了豪华度假村、开发了港口和机场、改善了海滩并扩展了海空航线。一切都在度假村内——酒店、海滩、购物和娱乐。度假者没有任何理由去探索岛屿的其他地方。
As in most places, those who have money live well indeed. Those who don't have money live at various levels of poverty. But here the poor greatly outnumber the wealthy. // 和大多数地方一样，有钱人确实过得很好。没钱的人生活在不同程度的贫困中。但在这里，穷人远远多于富人。
A visitor will find rich people living in apartments or Spanish houses at the seaside or in the countryside. Their servants might include a cook, a maid, and a nurse for the children. Most of the people live well below the poverty level. In towns, they live crowded together in tiny houses. Islanders make the best they can of what they have. Their homes are quite shabby. Sadly, most tourists never see this side of the Caribbean. // 游客会发现富人住在海边或乡下的公寓或西班牙式房屋里。他们的仆人可能包括厨师、女佣和照顾孩子的保姆。大多数人的生活水平远低于贫困线。在城镇里，他们拥挤地住在狭小的房子里。岛上居民尽力利用他们所拥有的一切。他们的家相当破旧。可悲的是，大多数游客从未看到加勒比海的这一面。
