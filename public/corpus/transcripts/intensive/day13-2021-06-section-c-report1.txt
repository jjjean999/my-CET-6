the use of driverless vehicles seemingly inevitable, mining companies in the vast Australian desert state of Western Australia are definitely taking the lead. // 随着无人驾驶车辆的使用似乎已不可避免，澳大利亚广阔的沙漠州——西澳大利亚州的矿业公司无疑正在引领潮流。
Iron ore is a key ingredient in steel-making. The mining companies here produce almost 300 million tons of iron ore a year. // 铁矿石是炼钢的关键原料。这里的矿业公司每年生产近3亿吨铁矿石。
The 240 giant autonomous trucks in use in the Western Australian mines can weigh 400 tons, fully loaded, and travel at speeds of up to 60 kilometers per hour. // 西澳大利亚矿山中使用的240辆巨型无人卡车满载时可达400吨，时速高达60公里。
They are a technological飞跃, transporting iron ore along routes which run for hundreds of kilometers from mines to their destinations. Here when the truck arrives at its destination, staff in the operation center direct it precisely where to unload. Vast quantities of iron ore are then transported by autonomous trains to ocean ports. // 它们是一项技术飞跃，沿着从矿区到目的地数百公里的路线运输铁矿石。当卡车到达目的地时，运营中心的工作人员精确指挥它在哪里卸货。然后大量铁矿石由自动驾驶列车运往海港。
Advocates argue these automated车辆 will change mining forever. It may only be five years before the use of automation technology leads to a fully robotic mine. // 支持者认为这些自动化设备将永远改变矿业。也许只需五年时间，自动化技术的使用就能带来完全机器人化的矿山。
A range of factors has pushed Western Australia's desert region to the lead of this automation revolution. These include the huge size of the mines, the scale of equipment and the repetitive nature of some of the work. Then there's the area's remoteness. At 502,000 square kilometers, it can sometimes make recruiting staff a challenge. // 一系列因素推动西澳大利亚沙漠地区在这场自动化革命中处于领先地位。这些因素包括：巨大的矿山规模、设备的规模以及某些工作的重复性。还有该地区的偏远性。面积达502,000平方公里，有时会使招聘员工成为一项挑战。
Another consideration is the risks when humans interact with large machinery. There are also the financial imperatives. The ongoing push by the mining corporations to be more productive and more efficient is another powerful driver in embracing automation technology. // 另一个考虑因素是人与大型机械交互时的风险。还有财务上的必要性。矿业公司持续追求更高生产力和更高效率的努力是拥抱自动化技术的另一个强大驱动力。
The concept of a fully autonomous mine is a bit of a misleading term, however. This is because the more technology is put into the field, the more people are needed to deploy, maintain and improve it. // 然而，"全自主矿山"这个概念有点误导性。这是因为投入现场的技术越多，就需要越多的人来部署、维护和改进它。
The automation and digitization of the industry is creating a need for different jobs. These include data科学家 and engineers in automation and artificial intelligence. // 行业的自动化和数字化正在创造对不同工作的需求。其中包括自动化和人工智能领域的数据科学家和工程师。
The mining companies claim automation and robotics present opportunities to make mining more sustainable and safer. Employees will be offered a career that is even more fulfilling and more rewarding. // 矿业公司声称自动化和机器人技术为使矿业更可持续、更安全提供了机会。员工将获得更有成就感、更有回报的职业发展。
Workers' unions have accepted the inevitability of the introduction of new technology, but they still have reservations about the rise of automation technology. Their main concern is the potential impact on remote communities. // 工会已经接受了新技术引入的必然性，但对自动化技术的兴起仍持保留态度。他们主要关注的是对偏远社区的潜在影响。
As automation spreads further, the question is how these remote communities will survive when the old jobs are eliminated. And this may well prove to be the most significant impact of robotic technology in many places around the world. // 随着自动化的进一步推广，问题是当旧的工作岗位被淘汰时，这些偏远社区将如何生存。这可能被证明是机器人技术在世界许多地方造成的最大影响。
