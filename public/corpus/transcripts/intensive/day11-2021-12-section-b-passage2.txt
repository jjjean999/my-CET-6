shows that a few moments of conversation with a stranger creates a measurable improvement in mood. // 研究表明，与陌生人交谈片刻就能带来可衡量的情绪改善。
But most of us are reluctant to start these conversations because we presume the opposite. // 但我们大多数人都不愿意主动开始这样的交谈，因为我们预设的结果恰恰相反。
In an experiment, commuters who talked to nearby strangers found their commute more enjoyable than those who didn't. // 在一项实验中，与附近陌生人交谈过的通勤者发现，他们的通勤过程比那些没有交谈的人更加愉快。
They were asked to predict whether they'd enjoy the commute more if they conversed with other people. Intriguingly, most expected the more solitary experience to be more pleasurable. // 他们被要求预测：如果与他人交谈，通勤是否会更加愉快。有趣的是，大多数人预期独处的体验会更令人愉悦。
Why is this? Social anxiety appears to be the problem. People's reluctance to start conversations with nearby strangers comes partly from underestimating others' interest in connecting. // 这是为什么？社交焦虑似乎是问题所在。人们不愿与附近的陌生人交谈，部分原因是低估了他人对交流的兴趣。
The sad thing is that people presume that a nearby stranger doesn't want to converse and don't start a conversation. // 可悲的是，人们预设附近的陌生人不想交谈，于是就不主动开启对话。
Only those who forced themselves to chat because it was required by the experiment found out what a pleasant experience it could be. // 只有那些因为实验要求而强迫自己聊天的人，才发现这原来是一种多么愉快的体验。
Human beings are social animals. Those who misunderstand the impact of social interactions may not, in some contexts, be social enough for their own well-being. // 人类是社交动物。那些误解社交互动影响的人在某些情境下可能社交不足，不利于自身幸福。
You should be chatting with the strangers you encounter. You may occasionally have a negative encounter that might stick in your memory. // 你应该与你遇到的陌生人交谈。你偶尔可能会遇到不愉快的经历，这些经历可能会留在你的记忆中。
This is because the human brain is biased to dwell on negative events. But starting conversations with strangers is still well worth the risk of rejection. // 这是因为人脑倾向于关注负面事件。但与陌生人开启对话仍然值得冒被拒绝的风险。
It may surprise you that conversing with strangers will make them happier too. The pleasure of connection seems contagious. // 让你惊讶的可能是，与陌生人交谈也会让他们更快乐。交往的快乐似乎具有传染性。
People who are talked to have equally positive experiences as those who initiate a conversation. // 被交谈的人与主动发起交谈的人拥有同样积极的体验。
