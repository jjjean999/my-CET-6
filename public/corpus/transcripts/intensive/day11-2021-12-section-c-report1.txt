​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​​​​​‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​​​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​​​​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​​‍‍​​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍‍​​​​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍​​​‍‍​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍‍​​​​​​‍‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍​​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍​​​‍​‍​‍‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍​‍‍‍​‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍​​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​‍​‍​‍​​‍‍​​‍​​‍​​‍‍​‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍‍​​​‍​‍‍​​‍​‍​‍​‍​​​‍​​​​‍​​‍‍​‍​​​​‍​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​‍‍​‍​​‍​‍​​​‍‍‍​​‍​‍‍​​‍‍​​‍​‍​​​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍​​‍‍‍‍​‍Talk to anyone who's a generation or two older, and they would most likely comment that children are more spoiled these days. // 和年长一两代的人聊天，他们很可能会说现在的孩子更娇惯了。
No one wants to have or be around demanding, selfish and spoiled children, those who get bad-tempered or silently生闷气 when they are not given everything they want immediately. // 没有人想要或愿意待在苛求、自私、被宠坏的孩子身边——那些一旦没有立即得到想要的一切就发脾气或闷闷不乐的孩子。
Paradoxically, the parents of such children encourage this demanding behavior in the mistaken belief that by giving their children everything they can, their children will be happy. // 矛盾的是，这类孩子的父母却在鼓励这种苛求行为，他们错误地认为只要给孩子一切所能给的，孩子就会快乐。
In the short term, perhaps they are right. But in the longer term, such children end up lonely, dependent, chronically dissatisfied and resentful of the parents who try so hard to please them. // 短期来看，也许他们是对的。但从长远来看，这样的孩子最终会变得孤独、依赖、长期不满，并怨恨那些竭力取悦他们的父母。
Undoubtedly, parents want to raise happy children who are confident, capable, and likable, rather than spoiled and miserable. // 毋庸置疑，父母希望培养出快乐、自信、能干、讨人喜欢的孩子，而不是被宠坏、痛苦不堪的孩子。
One factor hindering this is that parents can't or don't spend enough quality time with their kids and substitute this deficit with toys, games, gadgets and the like. // 阻碍这一目标的一个因素是：父母不能或不愿花足够的高质量时间陪伴孩子，而是用玩具、游戏、电子产品等来弥补。
Rather than getting material things, children need parents' devoted attention. The quantity of time spent together is less important than the content of that time. // 孩子需要的不是物质东西，而是父母的专注陪伴。在一起的时间长短不如时间的质量重要。
Instead of instantly satisfying their wishes, parents should help them work out a plan to earn things they'd like to have. This teaches them to value the effort as well as what it achieves. // 父母不应立即满足他们的愿望，而应帮助他们制定计划去争取自己想要的东西。这教会他们珍惜努力的过程以及努力所取得的成果。
Allow them to enjoy anticipation. Numerous psychological studies have demonstrated that children who learn to wait for things they desire are more likely to succeed in a number of ways later in life. // 让他们享受期待的过程。大量心理学研究表明，学会等待自己想要的东西的孩子在日后的人生中更有可能在多个方面取得成功。
One famous experiment in the 1960s involved 3- to 6-year-old children. They were given a choice between receiving a small reward, such as a cookie immediately, or if they waited 15 minutes, they could have two. // 20世纪60年代有一项著名实验，对象是3到6岁的孩子们。他们面临一个选择：立即得到一份小奖励（如一块饼干），或者等待15分钟后得到两块。
Follow up studies have found that those who chose to delay satisfaction are now more academically successful, have greater self-worth, and even tend to be healthier. // 追踪研究发现，那些选择延迟满足的孩子如今学业更成功、自我价值感更强，甚至往往更健康。
If they fail, children should be encouraged to keep trying rather than to give up if they really want the desired result. This teaches them how to handle and recover from disappointment, which is associated with greater success and satisfaction academically, financially and in personal relationships. // 如果失败了，只要孩子真的想要那个结果，就应该鼓励他们继续尝试而不是放弃。这教会他们如何应对和从失望中恢复过来，这与更大的成功和满意度相关——无论是在学业、经济方面还是在人际关系中。
And lastly, parents should encourage their children to look at life from other points of view, as well as their own. This teaches them to be understanding of and sympathetic towards others — qualities sure to take them a long way in life. // 最后，父母应该鼓励孩子从他人的角度以及自己的角度来看待生活。这教会他们理解和同情他人——这些品质必将让他们受益终生。
